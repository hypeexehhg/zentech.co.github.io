Welcome to Zentech Company

1. Who We Are

Zentech was established on May 7, 2023. We import high-quality goods, food, and electronics from international markets to customers across Sri Lanka.

2. Our Purpose

To provide the best service by delivering imported goods quickly and reliably, connecting global suppliers to local needs.

3. What We Do

  • Import electronics, food, and general goods
  • Islandwide delivery services
  • Wholesale supply to retailers
  • Reliable customer support
  • Focus on quality and efficiency

4. Terms & Conditions

  • Orders processed with full/partial payment
  • Delivery times vary but we prioritize speed
  • No returns unless damaged or defective
  • Warranty per manufacturer’s policy
  • We maintain transparency and confidentiality
  • Disputes resolved under Sri Lankan law

5. Our Target

To deliver top-quality imports fast and reliably across Sri Lanka, always exceeding expectations in speed and service.

6. Our Vision

To be Sri Lanka’s most trusted and fastest-growing import provider, bringing global products to every household and business.
