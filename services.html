Our Premium Services

Zentech proudly stands as a trusted name in Sri Lanka’s import-export industry, with a core focus on **electronic goods** and **consumer satisfaction**.

1. Importing Global Electronics

We bring world-class electronics from Japan, Korea, China, and Europe directly to the Sri Lankan market. Laptops, phones, TVs, smart gadgets—you name it, we import it.

2. Export of Local Products

Zentech isn’t just inbound—we export Sri Lankan excellence too. From spices and tea to small electronics, we bridge our island’s richness with the world.

3. Islandwide Delivery

Fast, reliable delivery across all 25 districts. Orders reach your doorstep, no matter how far—because our wheels never stop turning.

4. Wholesale Supply to Businesses

Retailers and resellers can count on Zentech for competitive pricing, bulk imports, and custom supply solutions tailored for their business.

5. Installation & After-Sales Support

We don't just sell electronics—we help install and maintain them. Our support team ensures your purchase keeps performing long after delivery.

6. Warranty & Return Policy

All electronics come with warranties per international standards. Damaged or faulty goods? We handle returns with care and honesty.

7. Business Consultation for Importers

New to importing? We guide small businesses through customs, shipping, taxes, and more. Our experience becomes your success.
